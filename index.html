Welcome to Plants vs. Zombies Game Guide!

Plants vs. Zombies is a tower defense game developed and published by Popcap Games. It was released on May 29, 2009.

This wiki and encylopedia of the game walks you through each plant, zombie, maps you will encounter, mechanics and hidden easter eggs of the game. A comprehensive documentation you will ever need!
