Zombie Yeti

720 dmg

High

Normal

Level Encountered:
Level 4-10 (After completing Adventure Mode)



Zombie Description

  • Zombie Yeti is a recurring character within the Plants vs. Zombies franchise. In tower defense games, Zombie Yeti acts as a rare encounter that drops rewards for the player if they're able to defeat him. In the shooter games, he acts as an ice based boss. Zombie Yeti doesn't appear in the initial playthrough of the game's Adventure Mode campaign; instead Zombie Yeti appears in subsequent replays of the gamemode, always appearing in Level 4-10 but sometimes appearing randomly in other levels and outside the gamemode such as in Survival Mode. When Zombie Yeti appears, he will slowly walk through the lawn for a short while before turning around in an attempt to retreat. If the player manages to defeat Zombie Yeti before he walks off the lawn, they will be rewarded 5 diamonds (4 upon repeat encounters).
