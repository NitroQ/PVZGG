Jalapeno Zombie

340 dmg

Low

Normal

Level Acquired:
ZomBotany(Minigame)

TJalapeno Zombie has a Chili pepper for a head. While he may be good for spicing up food, he also has the tendency to explode all over your lawn. It's a sad fact of the zombie apocalypse - food will be significantly blander and less spicy. But at least you're still alive (for now).

I can still get meat sticks though right? RIGHT?

Zombie Description

  • Jalapeno Zombie is similar to a normal Zombie, except that it has a Jalapeno for its head. It only appears in ZomBotany 2. A Jalapeno Zombie will explode after a set time, moves the same speed as a normal Zombie, destroying the entire lane and sometimes has a shorter fuse, making it dangerous. Like all other ZomBotany zombies, it does not have a Suburban Almanac entry.

Strategies

  • Its explosion clears an entire lane of plants, just like how a Jalapeno clears out a lane of zombies. It will detonate as soon as it reaches the fifth or sixth lane from the left unless it is not slowed or stopped and does not stop to eat plants. These zombies are usually found with other zombie types and are as tough as a Pole Vaulting Zombie. Try to kill them as quickly as possible or you may find your plants in an entire lane gone. Try to use Squash or other instant kills. Spikeweed and Spikerock can usually destroy a Jalapeno Zombie before it explodes. But if it is the only zombie there, you can also let your plants kill it.
  • Hypno-shrooms are a very bad idea around it, as when the Jalapeno Zombie is about to leave the screen, it explodes and takes out all plants in the lane. If there is an awake Hypno-shroom in that lane, remove it immediately. They are extremely dangerous if one of them detonates in a pool lane, s the pool lanes are usually very weak. Use a Tangle Kelp on the zombie to stop it from doing so. You can also use a Garlic to divert them into empty lanes. Just use an instant kill if all else fails. Take note that the fire will only destroy aquatic plants; any plant on top of Lily Pads are safe due to a glitch.
  • If you do not manage to kill it and it destroys your lane, it will leave your lane empty for any zombie and you might have to use a Lawn Mower or Pool Cleaner. However, an alternative could be put up a Wall-nut and let it distract the zombies until you can clear them all with an instant-kill (e.g. Squash) or replant the lane back.
