Umbrella Leaf

100

Normal

Fast

Level Acquired:
Level 5-6

"SPROING!" says Umbrella Leaf. "Didja like that? I can do it again. SPROING! Woo! That's me popping up to protect stuff around me. Yeah. Just like that. EXACTLY like that. Believe it."

Plant Description

  • Umbrella Leaf is the fifth defensive plant obtained in Adventure Mode and is unlocked after completing Level 5-6 making it the thirty-eighth plant overall. When planted, it protects adjacent plants from Bungee Zombies and Catapult Zombies and it can also bounce the zombies that Bungee Zombies drop during the final wave of every Roof level.

Strategies

  • Use these only on levels where Bungee Zombies and Catapult Zombies appear and place them around your most important plants. Each Umbrella Leaf covers a 3x3 square area centered on the Umbrella Leaf.
  • Plant Sunflowers in the back and on the next column put Umbrella Leaves on the second and fifth squares. Do the same for every third column after that, except every other time put them in the first and fourth squares instead to even out offensive plants. This strategy leaves the most room at the start of the level for offensive plants while leaving ample sun and is therefore best for setups that involve Kernel-pults or Cabbage-pults as starter offensive plants, and later progress onto Melon-pult.

Trivia

  • A landing Imp, a landing Pole Vaulting Zombie, a landing Dolphin Rider Zombie, a landing Snorkel Zombie, a van attack by Dr. Zomboss, a jumping or landing Pogo Zombie nor a landing Balloon Zombie will not be deflected by it.
  • If the player looks closely, it moves faster in the Zen Garden.
  • It is possible for a Bungee Zombie to steal an Umbrella Leaf if it has been planted after the Bungee Zombie has come down.
  • If it is inside a Pumpkin and its leaves expand, for a moment the main body can be seen through the Pumpkin before it goes back to normal.
  • When a basketball or Bungee Zombie attacks an area protected by more than one Umbrella Leaf, the Umbrella Leaf planted first will deflect it.
  • It is possible to get this plant in It's Raining Seeds, but this is extremely rare. This makes it the rarest plant to obtain in said mini-game.
  • The sound effect that the Umbrella Leaf makes when fending off zombies did not originate from the game but was a generic ricochet sound effect by Hanna-Barbera. This can only be heard when fending off Bungee Zombies.
  • Umbrella Leaf is currently the only Roof obtained plant to not reappear in Plants vs. Zombies 2 or its Chinese version.
