"I'm ready!" yells Squash. "Let's do it! Put me in! There's nobody better! I'm your guy! C'mon! Whaddya waiting for? I need this!"

Plant Description

  • Squash is a single-use plant in Plants vs. Zombies. When Squash spots a zombie in an area near him, he will stomp it for 1800 area damage.
  • When an enemy is within a 0.5 tile radius near him (both forward and backward), Squash becomes invincible, jumps, and lands near his target before despawning. The attack covers 1 tile, centered around his hit location.

Strategies

  • Squash can be considered to be an upgraded version of Potato Mine for one main reason: Unlike Potato Mine, Squash does not suffer from a lengthy arming time which would normally prevent Potato Mine from being used in the late levels - Unlike Potato Mine, Squash becomes invincible as soon as he spots something, allowing him to quickly neutralize or cripple hordes in a pinch, even if they are behind.
