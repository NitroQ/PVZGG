Tangle Kelp

25

Slow

Massive

Level Acquired:
Level 3-3

"I'm totally invisible," Tangle Kelp thinks to himself. "I'll hide here just below the surface and nobody will see me." His friends tell him they can see him perfectly well, but he'll never change.

Plant Description

  • Tangle Kelp is unlocked by completing Level 3-3. Tangle Kelp is a single-use aquatic plant, he can only be planted in water, but will instantly kill any zombie that he abducts. He drags the first zombie that makes contact with it underwater, killing the zombie regardless of health and vanishing after.

Strategies

  • Tangle Kelp's main advantage in comparison to other ground-based instant plants is the plant's relatively low cost, combined with fast recharge rate and independence from Lily Pad. Because of this, Tangle Kelp can be used as a specialized plant for guarding water lanes early on, acting like an economical, single-target sea mine that buys the player time to set up. Unfortunately, Tangle Kelp's limited effect plus his inability to be planted on land mean that he quickly loses his potential in the mid- to late-level.
